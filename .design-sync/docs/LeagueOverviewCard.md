LeagueOverviewCard summarizes a league: an initials avatar, name, a sport chip and role badge, a 2×2 stat grid (teams / players / events / divisions), and an optional recent-activity panel. League and team admins get a Manage action.

```tsx
<LeagueOverviewCard league={league} userRole="LEAGUE_ADMIN" recentActivity={activity} />
```
